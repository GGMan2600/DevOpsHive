What is a Site Reliability Engineer

A Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) is an IT professional focused on ensuring the reliability, scalability, and performance of software systems. They blend software engineering and operations skills to automate processes and keep services running smoothly. SREs work with development and operations teams on monitoring, incident response, and performance optimization.

Typical responsibilities include building and maintaining alerting and monitoring systems, troubleshooting production issues, and automating repetitive tasks. They also support capacity planning, disaster recovery, and performance tuning. Overall, SREs promote best practices that improve reliability, availability, and efficiency while fostering a culture of resilience and continuous improvement.
